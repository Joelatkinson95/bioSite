Hobbies

Travel

My favorite hobby is traveling. No matter what, I always want to have at least one trip booked in order to have something to look forward to and continue to see the world. In my senior year of college, I studied abroad in Manchester, England. This allowed me to not only live and experience the U.K, but also to take cheap and easy flights all over Europe on my weekends. I have visited 13 countries and counting. I love to travel around the U.S. as well. I enjoy visiting national parks and seeing the beauty that our country has to offer. My favorite place too visit is Hawaii. My husband and I go to Hawaii at least once a year and it is always somewhere that we look forward to visiting. My dream vacation that I must do before I die is visit a wildlife safari in Africa.
